METHODS TO PLAY

  1. Start the game by clicking the "Start" button.
  2. Moles will randomly pop up from holes on the game board.
  3. As soon as a mole appears,tap it as quickly as possible to "whack" it back into its hole.
  4. You'll earn points for each mole you successfully whack.
  5. The game typically has a time limit.
  6. The game ends when the time runs out and your score will be displayed.
